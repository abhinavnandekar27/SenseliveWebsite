SenseLive Indoor PoE Gateway SL2304

Safety, Security & Surveillance

SenseLive is a BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) to POE (Power over Ethernet) Gateway. It supports BLE 5.0, 2.4GHz WIFI, and Ethernet, offering both wireless and wired connectivity. Seamlessly connect to your cloud server via MQTT protocol. As a vital data bridge, SL2304 efficiently gathers nearby beacon advertising data and transmits it to your server via WIFI or Ethernet. Ideal for cost-effective, real-time location services, condition monitoring, asset tracking, and personal safety management.

Key Features

  • Dual Connectivity (BLE 5.0 and 2.4GHz Wi-Fi): Flexible and robust wireless communication.
  • POE Powered with Cascading: Simplifies power and network management, supporting up to 5 gateways.
  • Cloud Integration via MQTT: Efficient data transmission to the cloud.
  • Remote Management: Bi-directional communication for enhanced control.
  • Secure and Durable: Robust encryption and rugged ABS+PC casing.

Applications

  • Industrial Automation: Monitor and track assets and conditions in real time to optimize industrial operations.
  • Healthcare: Enhance patient safety and asset management within healthcare facilities.
  • Logistics: Track shipments and manage inventory with real-time data.
  • Retail: Improve customer experience and operational efficiency through real-time tracking.
