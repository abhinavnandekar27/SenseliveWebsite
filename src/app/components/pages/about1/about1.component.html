About Senselive

SenseLive is a pioneering technology company specializing in Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT) solutions and Industry 4.0 applications. The company is dedicated to helping industrial businesses transform their operations, enhance productivity, and reduce downtime through innovative IoT and AI-powered technologies.

SenseLive reached a significant milestone by successfully implementing its Plug-and-Play IoT architecture in 100 factories, revolutionizing their industrial processes.
